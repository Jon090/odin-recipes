Vegetable Lasagna

Description

Grandma brought out all the good stuff for this lasgna, except! Whats that?!? Grandma accidentally brought a freezer bag of coffee grounds instead of hamburger meat? (Why does that exist?) Silly grandma, at 98 years old, she's bound to make mistakes. Hopefully she remembers the hamburger next time she drives over..why is she still driving!!!

So what now? Well, luckily, you can still make an amazing lasgna without meat! Here, we have a vegetable lasgna. The big 3 make an appearance, Bell Peppers, Mushrooms, and Onions. I personally prefer an alfredo brocolli lasgna, but this version is tasty as well!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of wavy sheet noodles
  • More olive oil than you know exists
  • 47 onions
  • 15 bell peppers
  • 187 mushrooms
  • buncha tomatos to make your own pasta sauce from scratch
  • ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesean cheese

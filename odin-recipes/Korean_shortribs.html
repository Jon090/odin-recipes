Korean Shortribs

Description

While not being known for godzilla, or the creation of animae, Korea does have two cultural icons it can be proud of.

  1. K-popI have no idea what this is but i'm told its a big deal
  2. Korean Shortribs

Here we will be discussing the latter, the delicious delicious latter, I would climb a ladder to great to this latter. Tasty in all the best ways, flavor, texture, beef that falls off the bone. An excellent latter, agreed upon by all. I'm speaking for the Human race here.

Ingredients

  • Beef ribs, Korean style
  • Brown sugar, White Sugar, all colors of sugar really
  • Soy Sauce
  • Toasted sesame oil
  • Minced Onions and Garlic
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Place the beef ribs in slow cooker pot
  2. Cover it with hefty amounts of all other ingredients
  3. Slow cook for 672 hours at the lowest setting
  4. Eat as fast as possible!
